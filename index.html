About

We're a non-profit association.

Global Shapers Zurich is the Zurich Hub of the Global Shapers Community, a global network of hubs developed and led by young people who are exceptional in their potential, their achievements and their drive to make a contribution to their communities. Initiated by the World Economic Forum (WEF), the Global Shapers Community seeks to unite driven people between 20 and 30 years old who want to develop their leadership potential towards serving society. To that end, the hubs undertake local or national projects to improve their communities and the state of the world.

Apply

Become a member of our community.

We are looking for new members from a broad variety backgrounds who represent the top of their respective fields. Are you:

  • between 20-29 years old (at time of application)
  • living in or near Zurich (or willing to travel from nearby Cantons)
  • committed to working with peers to improve our community
  • interested in developing your leadership potential
  • ready to engage in the community and follow our Community Charter(s)

We seek diversity in our Hub – in terms of professional fields (arts & media, sports, politics, engineering & tech, finance & social impact), culture and origin, gender and sexual orientation, educational background, and more.

Are you keen to get involved?

Our recruitment takes place 4 times a year. Fill the form below. Selected candidates get invited for an informal meetup where we get to know each other. If you’re accepted, you’ll be on a 6 month probation period where you’ll demonstrate your active engagement.
